SAND POEM

the sand of your touch
first so warm and so much
feel its weight like a child
buried deep.
at the beach.
by siblings.


the comfort is surrounding
but the waves still are pounding
as grains wisp by and tickle my face
i cannot.
dig out.
by myself.


aint this risk the truest rush?
a timeless story that makes you blush
wrapped in thick sandy sun and embracing
oceanview.
you always.
by my side.

----------------------------------------


the sun steps into the water
friends help me up from the shore
go home, wash it off, try to sleep.
little crystals.
are stuck.
in my hair.


with time i would know
that you never find just one grain of sand
though crushed, old rocks keep close
a reminder.
everything.
is connected.


and that blown from afar
or caked hard where you are
there’s no castles
just the dunes.
and the reeds.
and the sea.

----------------------------------------


love is not just the sand
you can hold in your hand
it’s the land. -scape that grows
past the beaches.
beyond what.
we can know.


i still think the coast is beautiful
still cherish the sun and the water
yet i no longer bow at its altar
and the sand.
in my hair.
i put there.


incomplete but linked
unattached but connected
countless grains
complexity.
countless ways.
to be free.
